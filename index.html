WHO WE ARE

Founded by alumni of the GA Web Development Immersive program, our goal is to build a sense of community among students, recent graduates, and alumni. General Assembly Ladies (GAL) is an off-campus guild of GA with the purpose of convening women to share our experiences, network and support each other, and ensure greater diversity in the tech industry.

Join Us

Interested in starting a study group or presenting at a GAL meetup? The GAL Core Team is always looking for new speakers, resources, and members. Please email or DM us on Twitter or Instagram if you are interested in collaborating.

FAQs

Who can join GAL?
GAL is a collective and safe space intended for women and gender neutral/non-comforming graduates of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program. Allies are always welcome.
How can I join GAL?
It's so easy...
• Sign up for a Meetup account.
• Navigate to the GAL Meetup page.
• Click on "Join Us!" (under the banner).
How often do the GALs meet?
The GAL Core Team meets on a weekly basis to plan monthly events that promote collaboration, networking, and skill-sharing among its members.
As a GAL, do I have to attend a certain number of events?
GALs can attend as many events as they wish. However, each event is capped based on the venue capacity. Be sure to reserve your spot at our next event on our Meetup page.
Does GAL have a membership fee?
GAL membership is FREE; GALs do not pay to join the group or participate in GAL events. We are currently seeking venue and food sponsors in order to continue hosting free events.

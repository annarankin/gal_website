The General Assembly WDI Ladies

Our Mission

Created by General Assembly (GA) WDI alumni who want to impart dev bootcamp wisdom and learn from others, General Assembly Ladies (GAL) is an off-campus guild of GA with the purpose of getting together to chat about our shared experiences there, job seeking, and working in the tech industry.

Join Us

Are you interested in starting a study group? Do you know someone who would like to speak or present at a GAL meetup? The GAL Core Team is always looking for new resources and members. Please email or direct message us on Twitter or Instagram if you are interested in collaborating with us.

FAQs

Who can join GAL?
The GAL collective is intended for female and gender-neutral graduates of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program. Allies are always welcome.

How can I join GAL?
It's so easy...
• Sign up for a Meetup account.
• Navigate to the GAL Meetup page.
• Click on "Join Us!" (under the banner).

How often do the GALs meet?
The GAL Core Team meets about once a week to plan programming that will promote collaboration, networking, and skill-sharing between women at any stage of GA’s WDI life cycle. Our goal is to host at least one GAL event per month.

As a GAL, do I have to attend a certain number of events?
GALs can attend as many events as they wish. However, each event is capped based on the capacity of its venue. Be sure to reserve your spot at our next event on our Meetup page.

Does GAL have a membership fee?
GAL membership is FREE; GALs do not pay to join the group or participate in GAL events. We are currently seeking venue and/or food sponsors in order to continue hosting free events.
